Cloudflare Turnstile

Use Cloudflare Turnstile to fight against spam and bots on form submissions.

Check out how to integrate on the frontend and how to validate the token on the backend.

  1. Load the Turnstile library with the defer attribute if you load it in the <head> or without the attribute in the <footer>.
  2. Add your public key to data-sitekey to render the widget.

Do NOT expose the private secret key in your application. It needs to be kept private on your server invisible from all users.
